Biography

Zhenzhong Chen is currently a Professor at Wuhan University. He has more than 20 years academic and industrial experience in image and video processing, coding, communication, and assessment, where he has published more than 300 international journal or conference papers. He has been an SAE/LGE/AE of IEEE TCSVT, Associate Editor of IEEE TIP, JASIST, JVCIR, and Editor of IEEE IoT Newsletters. He has been active in ISO/IEC JTC 1/SC 29, ITU-T SG12/SG21, IEEE and AVS where he has developed ITU-T P.919/P.940/P.UXV, and has contributed over 300 technical proposals for AVC/H.264, HEVC/H.265, VVC/H.266, MPEG VCM/FCM, JPEG-AI, AVS1/2/3, IEEE 1857, etc. He has been an editor of ITU-T SG12, VQEG board member and Immersive Media Working Group Co-Chair, and Co-chair of China AVS Quality Assessment working group, and a Selection Committee Member of ITU Young Innovators Challenges. He was a recipient of the CUHK Young Scholars Dissertation Award, the CUHK Faculty of Engineering Outstanding Ph.D. Thesis Award, Microsoft Fellowship, Shenzhen Patent Award, etc.

He has received the fundings supports from National Oversea Talents Plan, NSFC Key Program and General Program, National Key R&D Program of China, NSF of Hubei Province Key Program, Wuhan Youth Science & Technology Plan, K. C. Wong Education Foundation, as well as industrial partners INRIA/IRISA (France), Tencent, Huawei, DFMC, etc. He has supervised more than 50 postgraduate students (PGS) and 100 final-year-project (FYP) undergraduate students, who have received more than 20 PGS National Scholarships, more than 10 Championships/Gold Medals/First-Class Prizes in International Challenges or National Contests, including CVPR CLIC 2018/2025, ICME Saliency 360, as well as ‘‘Challenge Cup’’ and ‘‘Internet +’’.
